Challenges

I had not coded for quite a while before this so getting into the swing of things once again was rather difficult. I had never used CSS grids before, I had mainly used floats and flex-box sometimes so once I wrapped my head around that and saw its power I was all good. I did struggle with the printable CSS media query too but I eventually understood it as I have never approached coding something in that way.

Beyond

I enjoyed styling many hover states on both the navigation and in the gallery. It was very interesting to see how difficult it was t style the drop shadow just right to appear as if material while on a screen, as well as tuning the transition times for the scale up to be just right. Content wise, condensiong my information into a structured template was a challenge for some unique projects and made me think about them more. Overall maintaining a cohesive design style and feel with whitespace, color and font was very fun.
