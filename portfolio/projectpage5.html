Paul Rand Lecture Poster

Graphic Design

Summary

For this project we were to design a lecture poster in the style of the designer that would be presenting. I chose Paul Rand, an American art director and graphic designer, best known for his corporate logo designs, including the logos for IBM, UPS, Enron, Morningstar, Inc., Westinghouse, ABC, and NeXT. He was one of the first American commercial artists to embrace and practice the Swiss Style of graphic design.
