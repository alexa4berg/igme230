The making of this site

My Audience/Goals


Concise copy, clean visuals and displaying high quality work is key. I want to show the type of work I want to do more of, displaying the type of designer I am and strive to become professionally. I expect my audiency to mainly be Co-op employers, full-time employers, anyone looking to view my work, teachers looking to hire/work with me, my family, me, and prospective students. They will most likely be viewin my work on either mobile or desktop.

Design Reasonings


I chose to go with the fonts and colors I did becuse I use them currently for my branding, so if I did want to use this site it would be cohesive in design style and structure. I chose the purpe beacause I like the color purple and thought this purple was a bit more energetic and exciting than others I've seen. I chose the font Playfair Display because I've grown to love the combination of serif and sans serif and the use of both is not too common online (standing out once again)
