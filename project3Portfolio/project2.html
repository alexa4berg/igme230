Supply Management for Diabetics


I’m designing a personal life management system to solve lack of physical organization for diabetics

View Deck

Overview


Having diabetes means having a lot of supplies and information to keep track of.

From insulin, pods and meters, to syringes, test strips and snacks for low blood sugar, there is a lot you can’t risk mismanaging when it’s your health at stake

I’m aiming to create a management system for diabetics that goes beyond viewing data and inputing numbers.

Creating an experience that helps a user better organize the physical aspects of their diabetic lifestyle and provide valuable assurance
