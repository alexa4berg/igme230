Paul Rand Lecture Poster


Designing a new lecture poster for, and inspired by, one of the greatest graphic designers of all time

Tools: Illustrator

Overview


After choosing from a list of designers, I was to design a lecture poster as if he were to be coming to RIT to speak and publication was needed. All detiled info was provided in order to keep the focus on the designing. Inspired by his bold use of color and block, industrial play style, I created this poster for Paul Rand
